About My Journey

I'm a passionate Telecommunication Engineer and IoT Developer with over 10 years of experience in building intelligent systems and smart solutions. My journey started with a fascination for how things communicate wirelessly, leading me to specialize in IoT, embedded systems, and telecom infrastructure.

Throughout my career, I've successfully delivered 25+ projects ranging from smart city solutions to home automation systems, always with a focus on innovation, reliability, and user experience.

Beyond development, I'm deeply committed to STEAM education, mentoring the next generation of engineers and tech enthusiasts. I believe in sharing knowledge and creating accessible pathways for others to enter the exciting world of technology.
