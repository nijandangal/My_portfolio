Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer IoT development, embedded systems consulting, telecom infrastructure design, project management, and STEAM education training. Each project is customized to your specific needs.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small IoT projects typically take 2-4 weeks, while larger systems may take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

Do you offer remote collaboration?

Yes! I work with clients worldwide using modern collaboration tools. Remote project management is one of my specialties, ensuring smooth communication and timely delivery.

What about post-project support?

All projects include documentation and basic support for 30 days after completion. Extended support packages are available for long-term maintenance and updates.

Do you provide training?

Yes, I offer workshops and training sessions on IoT development, embedded systems, and related technologies. Both online and in-person training available.
